Aims & Scope

Biohelikon: Cancer and Clinical Research is an international, peer reviewed open access online journal publishes ground breaking discoveries that will advance the Cancer and Oncology. This open access journal publishes manuscripts on a broad range of topics that explore basic, translational and clinical work in cancer research.

The Biohelikon Cancer and Clinical research will provide a platform for the researchers, physicians, oncologists working in the field of cancer biology and oncology. The Journal publishes research, Review, Case Reports, Editorials, Perspective, and clinical pictures type of manuscripts that will explore cancer research.

Publication Fees

Biohelikon: Cancer and Clinical Research use a business model in which authors or research sponsors need to pay article processing charges which includes- peer view processing, journal production(PDF, HTML formats), online hosting, copy editing and more. The publication fee for Open Access in this journal is $195. If author cannot pay the full amount, we're happy to provide relevant discounts and may provide waiver on the article processing charges.
