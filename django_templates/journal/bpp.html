Biohelikon: Plant Physiology

Aims & Scope

Biohelikon: Plant Physiology is an open access international journal that deals with physiology of plants. Scope of the journal includes study of all internal activities of plants including molecular and cell biology, genetics, hormones and environmental biology.

Salient Features

Your article will be usually processed with in 45 days of submission with assignment of reviewer after two days of submission.

Why Open Access?

Open access gives a worldwide audience larger than that of any subscription-based journal and thus increases the visibility and impact of published works. It also enhances indexing, retrieval power and eliminates the need for permissions to reproduce and distribute content.
