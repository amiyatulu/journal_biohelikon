Biohelikon: Cell Biology

Aims & Scope

Cell biology is a scientific discipline that deals with study of cell. It is an integrative science that brings together biochemistry, molecular biology, developmental biology and physiology. Cells are made up of enormous complexity, with millions of interactions taking place. Techniques like cloning, stem cells provide exciting possibilities in the field of treating disease and injury.

Salient Features

Your article will be usually processed with in 45 days of submission with assignment of reviewer after two days of submission.

Why Open Access?

Open access gives a worldwide audience larger than that of any subscription-based journal and thus increases the visibility and impact of published works. It also enhances indexing, retrieval power and eliminates the need for permissions to reproduce and distribute content.



We aim to bring important work to a wide range of audience and therefore publish articles that advance our understanding of cellular biology.
