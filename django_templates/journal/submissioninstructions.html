Manuscript Submission process of Biohelikon includes three steps:

Step 1 (Registration):
First of all you need to create an account by signing up. This step is not required if you have created an account before. Then you can login into your account, by clicking on Log In button.

Step 2 (Form Submission):
After login, click on the Manuscript button, to submit your manuscript.
You will be given a form with following fields, which you need to fill.

  • Title of the article
  • Authors
  • Authors affiliation
  • Corresponding author name
  • Corresponding author Email
  • Corresponding author city
  • Corresponding author country
  • Abstract of the article / Introduction

Then click on the Submit button to follow Step 3:

Step 3 (Uploading):
Please upload all the files including source file, images, equations and references.
Please mention the article type (e.g. Editorial,Research etc.) in your source file. Source file can be of doc or docx format.
In order to make the peer review blind, please don't mention author details in the source file.
Body reference numbers should be kept within square brackets [  ] and should be in increasing order.
References can be of MLA, APA or Chicago format.
Mention the figure and table legends below each figure and table.
Files can be uploaded in batch. You can remove the file by clicking on the delete button any time before saving your files.


A manuscript id will be assigned to you. You can contact us any time regarding status of the article under process.
You can view your manuscript in Manuscript tab, after signing in. You can also update and upload the updated manuscript any time in response to reviewer remarks. You are advised to submit your revised manuscript based upon the reviewer comments within 15 days.
